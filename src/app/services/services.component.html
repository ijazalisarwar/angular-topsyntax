FLOW OF WORK

1. Ideation

The most important phase requires a lot of experimentation. We tend to focus on all the steps of ideation, start from design, building a prototype and keep testing until we find the best framework for your business. We define the metrics that will help us to get the best ideas throughout the project to make sure it generates 3x ROI.

01 Discovery

Get complete visibility into your Business plan alongwith the complete idea of resources, keep track of changes occurring at each and every level. Get an idea of cloud, and serverless infrastructure going to be involved in setting up a strong foundation with accurate data and relationship views for the complete project.

02Revisit the Ideas

Many ideas fail that once we plan to move on, we don’t go for an unbiased re-evaluation of the idea. We always have a parallel team for each involved team to revisit the ideas, discovery and provide the insights at a very initial stage.

2. Product Strategy

A product strategy draws from the ultimate vision of the product. By setting a product strategy, you can determine the direction of your product efforts. Product Strategy emerges from experimentation towards an aligned goal set by the key decision makers in the business.

01 Product Backlog

A well-prioritized and healthy agile backlog is the backbone of a product. It does not only make releases and iteration planning easier but it also broadcasts all the things your resources must know to spend time on, taking care of all the internal work that the customer might never notice. This helps set expectations with stakeholders and other teams, especially when they tend to expand the business and that’s where you team can stand up and deliver the high quality work.

02Roadmap

A product roadmap is a high-level visualed summary that maps out the vision and direction of your product offering over time. Creating a product roadmap is a multi-step process, as you will first need to align the vision and strategic goals for your product by mentioning the its strategic reason, determining your audience and tailoring the roadmap to resonate with your product’s strategic themes. If you stay flexible throughout this process, it helps you in building the actual roadmap.

3. Product Iterations

Every success story demands to have two to three workable, communicated and agreed upon Product Iterations. This helps team to have a broader vision and jolting the ideas in the running time.

01 Planning & Progress

A product depends upon the well thought planning proceduer before getting it ready for the production. All steps are being monitored at each phase by different members of our team on daily basis.

02Development Progress

Development must match the framework designed for the product to keep it on track. Attention to detail is must while keeping the tab on progress in developmental stage.

03QA

Quality Assurance team tracks test results and project activity to increase productivity and reduce risk. Our Modern and flexible QA team is helping developers to meet the deadlines while assuring that quality gets delivered as we promise.

03Releases

For any efficient team, it's best to release easily and often. Our team believes in making release a natural part of the agile culture by building a modular architecture. APIs can be tested automatically with each build to ensure compatibility and reduce risk in the software release.

4. Maintenance

Maintenance of a product is equally important as Web Application Development. It keeps software solutions healthy to deal with all time challenging and changing technical business environments globally.

01 Bug Fixing

Our QA team makes sure that they don’t bother you for this purpose. Having said that, if you come up with anything went missing, or any problem which arises at the later stage, you will find us doing this part with a smile on our face.

02Optimization

The purpose of optimization is to achieve the “best” final product relative to a set of prioritized criteria or constraints finalised with our clients. This includes maximising factors at each stage i.e strength, productivity,efficiency, reliability, longevity, and utilization. We want to make sure that each visit by your customer to your website is successful for you.
